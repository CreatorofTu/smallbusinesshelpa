Set up instruction manual

Now for the fun part — it's time to add ai into your business.

  1. You or an employee needs to walk around your business and write down everything. Yes, everything. (E.g. doors, bathrooms, tables, speakers, spotlights, fireplaces, coat racks — literally everything.) It's essential for you to get the most out of our ai.
  2. Once done naming and counting everything (e.g. 3 tables and 12 chairs and 2 speakers), we will process your list. Click submit.
  3. Place a sticker on everything in this list — it'll be everything. Make sure the stickers are easily viewable.
  4. Once done placing stickers, take your phone and scan every sticker, then tap the name of the item. The count will decrease as you go (3 tables mentioned, 2 tables left, 1 table left as you scan then assign).

Tip: every sticker has its number printed on it — typing the number in this app works just as well as scanning.
